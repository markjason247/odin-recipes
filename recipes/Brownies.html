Brownies

Brownies:

A chocolate brownie or simply a brownie is a square or rectangular chocolate baked confection. Brownies come in a variety of forms and may be either fudgy or cakey, depending on their density. Brownies often, but not always, have a glossy "skin" on their upper crust. These bite-sized crunchy and crumbly cookies are sweet and addicting.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1/3 cup chopped cashews

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit. Grease and line a 9-inch square pan. Set aside.
  2. In a bowl, cream butter and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs, one at a time, while beating continuously.
  4. Add the rest of the ingredients except half of the nuts. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Pour into prepared pan.
  6. Spread evenly.
  7. Sprinkle remaining nuts on top.
  8. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes. Cut into squares.

    9. yield: 16 squares
