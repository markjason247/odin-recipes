Polvoron

Polvoron:

Polvoron is a Filipino shortbread cookie made with toasted flour, powdered milk, butter, and sugar. These bite-sized crunchy and crumbly cookies are sweet and addicting. Polvoron is commonly spelled pulburon or polboron. It is also referred to as Filipino milk candies.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups Softasilk cake flour
  • 2 cups powdered milk
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup toasted pinipig, ground
  • 1 cup butter oe margarine, melted

STEPS

  1. Toast flour in moderate heat until light brown, stirring constantly.
  2. In a bowl, mix together the toasted flour, milk, sugar, pinipig and melted butter or margarine.
  3. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Using a round or oval polvoron molder, shape the mixture.
  5. Wrap in japanese paper or cellophane.

    6. yield: 5 dozens
